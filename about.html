the story so far

From boardrooms to living rooms.

Samir is a speaker and coach on human relationships, leadership, handling stress, and building high-performing teams. His workshops and conference talks have taken him across Asia, the Americas, Europe, Africa, Australasia, and the Middle East.

For nearly a decade he hosted a show on COLORS TV made to encourage ordinary viewers — more than 15 million of them. The stories those viewers wrote in still shape the way he coaches.

He has coached senior executives at PwC, Mott MacDonald, and Egis, and worked with WHO, the World Bank, UNDP, St. Stephen’s College, and non-profits including IJM and EFICOR. His ELEVATE program grew out of that work.

He wrote Getting There and Staying There, now in three languages. He serves on several non-profit boards and offers spiritual counsel to Dwaar, a community in Delhi. And first of all: a devoted husband, and a father of two.
