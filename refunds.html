Refunds & cancellations

The policy for every order placed on samirdeokuliar.co — in plain language.

the short version
  • Cancel free within 24 hours, as long as your order hasn’t shipped.
  • Damaged, defective, or wrong books? Tell us within 7 days of delivery — we’ll replace them free, or refund you.
  • Refunds go back to your original payment method within 7–10 working days.
  • E-books, digital downloads, and signed copies can’t be refunded.

1. Cancelling an order

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it, provided it hasn’t shipped yet. Once an order has shipped, it can no longer be cancelled — but the returns policy below may still cover you.

2. Returns

Returns are accepted when something went wrong with your order:

  • The books arrived damaged or defective, or you received the wrong item.
  • Requests must be made within 7 days of delivery, with photos of the issue.

3. Refunds

Once your return is approved, the refund is processed to your original payment method within 7–10 working days. Payments made through Razorpay, PayPal, or Stripe are credited on those providers’ own timelines.

4. What can’t be refunded

  • E-books, digital downloads, and signed copies.
  • Shipping fees, once an item has been dispatched.

5. Replacements

If a book arrives damaged or incorrect, we’ll send a replacement at no extra charge once the issue is verified — usually all it takes is a photo.

6. Questions about an order?

Write to care@samirdeokuliar.co or call +91 96672 45010 — include your order number and we’ll sort it out quickly.
