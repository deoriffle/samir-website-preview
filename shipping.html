Shipping

How orders from samirdeokuliar.co reach you — in India and worldwide.

the short version
  • Orders are packed and processed within 2–5 business days.
  • Delivery within India takes 5–10 business days, depending on where you are.
  • International delivery typically takes 10–21 business days.
  • You get tracking details by email the moment your order ships.

1. Within India

Orders are processed within 2–5 business days and delivered in 5–10 business days, depending on the destination. We ship with trusted courier partners such as India Post, DTDC, and Blue Dart, and email you the tracking details as soon as your order is dispatched.

2. International orders

International orders ship via standard or express global courier services and typically arrive within 10–21 business days, depending on customs clearance. Any customs duties or import taxes are the buyer’s responsibility.

3. Delays

Once a parcel is with the courier, its pace is in their hands — we aren’t responsible for delays caused by courier companies, customs, or unforeseen events. Your tracking link is always the fastest way to see where things stand.

4. Getting your address right

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. Incorrect details can cause delays or non-delivery, and we can’t redirect a parcel once it’s on its way.

5. Questions about a shipment?

Write to care@samirdeokuliar.co or call +91 96672 45010 with your order number — we’ll track it down.
